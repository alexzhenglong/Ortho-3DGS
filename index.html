Ortho4D-GS: Robust Orthophoto Generation in Weakly Dynamic Scenes

Junxing Yang, Cai Alex, etc.

[Paper] [Poster] [Code] [Video]

Abstract

This work proposes Ortho4D-GS, a robust orthophoto generation method based on enhanced 3D Gaussian Splatting, designed to handle weakly dynamic scenes in UAV photogrammetry. Our approach integrates DINO features and appearance modeling to reduce ghosting and improve geometric consistency.
