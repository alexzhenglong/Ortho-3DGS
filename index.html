Ortho4D-GS: Robust Orthophoto Generation in Weakly Dynamic Scenes

Junxing Yang, Cai Alex, Others

[Paper] [Poster] [Code] [Video]

Abstract

We propose Ortho4D-GS, a robust orthophoto generation method using enhanced 3D Gaussian Splatting. By integrating DINO features and appearance modeling, our method effectively reduces ghosting artifacts and enhances geometric consistency in UAV photogrammetry, even under weakly dynamic scenes.
