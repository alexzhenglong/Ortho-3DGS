Abstract

We present Ortho-3DGS, a novel approach for true digital orthophoto generation from unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) imagery. Our method introduces depth-regulated 3D Gaussian Splatting, which leverages geometric constraints and depth priors to ensure spatial accuracy and reduce ghosting artifacts caused by dynamic objects and perspective distortions. Experiments on urban UAV datasets demonstrate that Ortho-3DGS significantly improves both geometric fidelity and visual quality compared with conventional photogrammetric methods.
